How Apriori Works

Step by Step
🔍
Scan Items
Count every item across all 9,835 transactions. Keep items appearing in ≥1% of baskets (min support threshold).
🔗
Combine Pairs
Generate candidate pairs from frequent items. Prune any pair where an item is infrequent — the core Apriori principle.
📐
Calculate Metrics
Compute Support (how often), Confidence (reliability), and Lift (strength vs random chance) for every rule.
✅
Extract Rules
Keep rules with Lift >1.0 — meaning items are bought together MORE than by chance alone. These are actionable insights!
